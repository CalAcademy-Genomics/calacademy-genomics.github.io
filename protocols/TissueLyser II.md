# Sample Homogenization with the TissueLyser II

{: .caution }
THIS IS A DRAFT PROTOCOL.

{: .caution }
Before using the TissueLyser II for the first time, consult CCG staff for proper training.

{: .note }
Homogenization settings will vary considerably depending on sample type, preservation method, and the type of homogenization beads used. Some experimentation will be required to find the best method for your samples.

### Protocol

Lift up the black plastic hood covering the wiggle mechanism.  Loosen and remove the black knob, then remove the large, plastic hold–down cap that covers and holds down the screw-cap tubes.  Insert the standard screw-cap tubes in the TissueLyser’s tube holding ring. Distribute all tubes symmetrically and use empty tubes to balance as necessary.


Slide the large, black plastic hold-down cap over the center bolt and align the cap so it slides down the ‘anti-rotation’ pin. The hold-down cap must make contact with the top of the aluminum wiggle mechanism – not just the tops of the tube caps.  Screw on and hand-tighten the black knob firmly.


Swing down the black plastic shield over the wiggle mechanism.  This protects the user from contact with the mechanism during operation and helps contain anything should it break free.
